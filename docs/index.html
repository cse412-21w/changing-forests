10,000 years ago,

There were 6 billion hectares of forest.

That’s 57% of the habitable land on Earth.

Let’s put this into perspective…

1 billion hectares is equivalent to the size of the U.S.

120 years ago,

We were down to 5.1 billion hectares and the forest only covered 48% of habitable land.

Present

38% of habitable land is covered by forest. That’s a loss of over 1 billion hectares since the 1900s, leaving us left with 4 billion hectares.

Our Forests are Changing.
